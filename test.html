Tractor price starts from ₹ 2.44 lakh and goes up to ₹ 72 lakh in India. However, the tractor prices vary based on power and features. In India, the lowest tractor price is the Swaraj Code. This 11 HP tractor is priced between ₹2.44 lakh and ₹2.49 lakh, making it suitable for basic farming tasks. On the other hand, the John Deere 5130 M, a 130 HP model, is the most expensive tractor in India. Its price starts at Rs. 68,00,000. Farmers can choose from a wide range of horsepower(HP) options, from compact 11 HP tractors for basic tasks to powerful 100 HP models for demanding fieldwork. Some of the leading tractor brands in India are Mahindra, Sonalika, John Deere, Eicher, New Holland and Swaraj. These brands offer a wide range of tractors, including 2WD, 4WD, and mini models. A few popular tractors are the Mahindra 575 DI XP Plus, Eicher 380 4WD Prima G3, Massey 241 Dynatrack, New Holland 3630 TX Super Plus+, and Sonalika Tiger DI 60 CRDS. Electric tractors are also gaining popularity in India. One such popular model is the Sonalika Tiger Electric, priced between Rs. 5.77 Lakh - Rs. 6.14 Lakh. It is a 15 HP tractor with a battery capacity of 250-350 A. The battery can be fully charged in 10 hours at home and in 4 hours with a fast-charging system. This model offers a sustainable alternative to diesel tractors. ============================= Tractor price starts from ₹ 2.44 lakh and goes up to ₹ 72 lakh in India.
