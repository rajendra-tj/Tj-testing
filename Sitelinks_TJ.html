Swaraj 855 FE Expert Review

“The Swaraj 855 FE is built for heavy-duty farming tasks with features that balance power, efficiency, and durability. Its high torque delivery makes it reliable for pulling and ploughing, while the 6-year warranty adds long-term value. The tractor supports a wide range of implements, from rotavators to threshers, and offers dependable performance with low maintenance.”

Overview

The Swaraj 855 FE is a versatile tractor designed for both heavy farm operations and transport needs. Powered by a 3-cylinder engine, it delivers a steady pulling force for tasks like ploughing, sowing, and threshing. Its high torque capacity ensures efficient work even in harder soils. A wheelbase designed for stability allows smooth performance with heavy implements, while the 2000 kg hydraulic lift offers flexibility for modern tools.

The tractor comes with oil-immersed brakes for reliable stopping power and multiple gearbox options to suit varied operations. Farmers benefit from a large 62-litre fuel tank, which reduces downtime during critical farming seasons. An IPTO option makes it easier to control PTO-driven implements without frequent gear changes. With an affordable price range and long warranty support, the Swaraj 855 FE is a strong option for farmers who want durability with lower upkeep costs.

Engine & Performance

  • With 49.3 HP, the Swaraj 855 FE handles field operations like ploughing and cultivating without stress, even in rough soil conditions.
  • The water-cooled system prevents overheating during summer or extended use, keeping the engine reliable across seasons.
  • A 3-stage oil bath air filter keeps dust and impurities away, extending engine life and reducing the need for frequent servicing.
  • Its high torque of 205 Nm ensures the tractor can pull loaded trailers on uneven ground and manage heavy implements without drop in power.
  • Its 3478 cc engine displacement delivers steady power output, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications.

Transmission & Gearbox

  • Two gearbox choices are available: 8F+2R or 12F+3R, giving flexibility for various tasks and terrains.
  • Speed options range from 3.1 km/h to over 30 km/h, allowing slow precision work in fields and faster transport runs.
  • The IPTO system lets operators change PTO speeds without halting the tractor, increasing productivity with tools like sprayers or pumps.
  • Double clutch technology improves convenience by allowing smooth gear changes while keeping PTO-driven tools active.
  • The constant mesh setup ensures smoother gear shifting and reduces wear, extending the working life of the transmission.

Comfort & Safety

  • Oil-immersed brakes give smoother stops, stay cooler during long hours, and require less servicing than dry brakes.
  • A comfortable seat and simple control placement reduce operator fatigue, helping in longer field shifts.
  • Improved LED lamps and tail lamps ensure better visibility during late evening work, reducing safety risks in low light.
  • The stronger fender design gives additional protection to tractor parts and contributes to longer durability.
  • Power steering keeps handling light, even when the tractor is attached with heavy-mounted implements.

Hydraulics & PTO

  • With a lifting capacity of 2000 kg, the Swaraj 855 FE can manage heavy tools like MB or reversible ploughs, cultivators, and rotavators easily.
  • Depth and position control ensures accurate soil preparation, giving uniform seed placement and efficient land use.
  • The PTO output of 42.9 HP allows operation of multiple tools like threshers, pumps, and seed drills without performance drops.
  • Its linkage system is designed for stability, keeping implements steady during uneven ground operations.

Fuel Efficiency

  • A 62-litre fuel tank supports long hours in the field without interruptions, ideal for sowing or harvesting days when downtime is costly.
  • The engine tuning is designed to optimize diesel use, making it cost-effective while still giving adequate pulling strength.
  • Even with heavier implements attached, the Swaraj 855 FE maintains balanced fuel usage, which reduces overall expenses.
  • Farmers save on fuel costs during commercial applications like haulage, where tractors often run for extended hours.

Implement Compatibility

  • The Swaraj 855 FE works efficiently with heavy-duty implements like reversible ploughs, cultivators, and rotavators.
  • PTO-driven machines like threshers, sprayers, and seed drills can be operated smoothly without performance lag.
  • Its lifting power supports advanced tools for modern mechanized farming, making it versatile across cropping patterns.
  • Farmers using trailers and trolleys for haulage benefit from its torque strength and stable balance during transport.
